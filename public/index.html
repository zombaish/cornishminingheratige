The Copper Kingdom

⚒

Deep-Lode Extraction

During the 18th and 19th centuries, Cornwall’s engineering innovations transformed global mining operations, establishing the region as the undisputed capital of copper and tin extraction.

⚙

High-Pressure Steam

Central to this industrial success was the high-pressure steam engine developed by local pioneers like Richard Trevithick, allowing miners to extract resources from unprecedented depths.

🌍

Global Export

The landscape of Cornwall underwent a radical transformation. The remains of engine houses and ports reflect a culture of innovation that exported mining expertise across the world.
